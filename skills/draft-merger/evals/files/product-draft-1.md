Product Name: AirBreeze Pro Portable Fan

The AirBreeze Pro is a compact, rechargeable portable fan designed for people on the go. With its powerful 3-speed motor and 360-degree rotation, it delivers refreshing airflow wherever you need it.

Key Features:
- Battery life: up to 8 hours on low speed
- USB-C charging
- Quiet operation under 40dB
- Foldable design fits in backpack
- Weight: 280g

The fan is perfect for commuters, outdoor activities, and travel. It comes with a built-in power bank function, allowing you to charge your phone while the fan is running.
